HOW TO PLAY

🎮 Use ARROW KEYS to move your snake

🍎 Collect food to grow longer and score points

🏃 Speed increases with levels:

⚡ Level 1: Normal Speed (0-49 points)

⚡ Level 2: 2x Speed (50-99 points)

⚡ Level 3: 3x Speed (100-149 points)

🏆 Win the game by reaching 150 points!

❌ Game Over if you hit walls or yourself

⏸️ Press P to pause/resume the game
